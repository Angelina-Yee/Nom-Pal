Nyan Aung

Nom-Pal started as a way for us, two college students, to budget our spendings for grocery and reduce waste. We wanted to make sure, we utilize all the items in our pantry when making new recipes, without having to rush to a nearby grocery store and spend more money. Sometimes, there are grocerys that go bad because we are not sure of what is left in the very back of our pantry or fridge. Nom-Pal is our pal that welcomes college students alike, and all the foodies and home chefs, to cook on a budget and minimizie space.
